From Business Analysis to Web Development: My Experience with Kodego's Bootcamp

by Lee Olivier

As an experienced business analyst, I recently decided to make a career shift into web development. After researching various options, I decided to join a fullstack web development bootcamp course offered by Kodego.

I have to say, the experience has been fantastic. The course is well-structured and covers a wide range of topics, from basic HTML and CSS to more advanced concepts like JavaScript and React.

One of the things I appreciate most about the course is the hands-on approach. We spend a lot of time working on projects and building our own web applications, which has been invaluable in helping me understand how everything fits together and how to apply what I'm learning to real-world scenarios.

The instructors are also top-notch. They are all experienced developers themselves, and they are able to explain complex concepts in a clear and easy-to-understand way. They also do a great job of providing individualized support and feedback, which has helped me to make steady progress.

In addition to the course material itself, Kodego also provides a great community of students and alumni, who are always willing to help and offer support. This has been extremely valuable in helping me to stay motivated and on track with my learning.

Overall, I couldn't be happier with my decision to join a fullstack web development bootcamp course from Kodego. It's been an intense but rewarding experience and I'm looking forward to applying my new skills in my next role.

If you're thinking about a career shift into web development, I highly recommend checking out Kodego's bootcamp course. It will provide you with a solid foundation in all the key technologies and give you the hands-on experience you need to be successful in this field.
